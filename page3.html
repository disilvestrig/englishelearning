PHRASAL VERBS

There are 4 types of phrasal verbs

  • Verb + preposition.The preposition in turn holds a noun or a pronoun and forms an indirect complement with this(ex. : Dad's looking for his paper)
  • Verbs + adverbial particle which do not hold an object complement. (ex.: All the lights have gone out .).
  • Verbs + adverbial particle that hold an object complement. If the complement is a noun, it may precede or follow the adverbial particle. But if it is a pronomr, it always precedes the adverbial particle. (ex.: Don't put out that light).
  • Verbs followed by adverbial particle + preposition . (ex.: Do you get on with your neighbors?).
